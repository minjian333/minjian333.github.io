I recently completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Mahesh Viswanathan, and I will join Nanyang Technological University as a postdoctoral researcher working with Luke Ong.

My research has always been algorithmic in nature, with a focus on algorithms for concurrent programs, dynamic analysis, and model checking.

More recently, a growing research focus of mine is how to enhance coding agents so that they can generate more correct concurrent programs.

My work has been recognized with a SIGPLAN Research Highlight, a CSF Distinguished Paper Award, and a POPL ACM SIGPLAN Distinguished Paper Award.
